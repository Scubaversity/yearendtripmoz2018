Mozambique
Year End Trip

Thursday, 29 - Sunday 02 Dec

The legendary Scubaversity
Year End Trip!

Included in the packages are supers (except one night on the town), eat as much Bacon as you like Scubaversity Breakfasts, a braai and then of course the year end function with proper cutlery, fancy tables, decorations, as many cocktails as you want on the beach but most importantly good times & all you guys!

You have the option of staying in a Tent or a Wooden Hut.
For those in Tents remember to bring your own bedding with as they only have two beds with mattresses. The Wooden Huts have two beds with bedding as well as electric lighting.
